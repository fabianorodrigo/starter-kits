Transfers a NFT {{ metadata["symbol"] }} from one account to another. The caller is responsible to confirm that the recipient is capable of receiving the NFT or it may be permanently lost
Makes a Safe Tranfer of a NFT {{ metadata["symbol"] }} from one account to another. By "Safe" it means that the recipient is a smart contract that is capable of receiving NFTs. This is confirmed by calling the `onERC721Received` function on the recipient and the returned value has to be `bytes4(keccak256("onERC721Received(address,address,uint256,bytes)"))`.
